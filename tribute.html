Alia Bhatt

The Bollywood Sensation

Alia Bhatt, the versatile actress known for her stellar performances in Bollywood.

Timeline of Alia Bhatt's Life

  • 1993: Born on March 15th in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.
  • 2012: Makes her debut in Karan Johar's film "Student of the Year".
  • 2014: Receives critical acclaim for her performance in "Highway".
  • 2016: Stars in "Udta Punjab" and wins numerous awards.
  • 2018: Shines in the critically acclaimed film "Raazi".
  • 2020: Launches her own production company, Eternal Sunshine Productions.
  • 2022: Wins the Filmfare Award for Best Actress for her role in "Gangubai Kathiawadi".
  • 2023: Expands her production company's portfolio with successful projects in film and digital media.
  • 2024: Announces collaboration with international filmmakers for upcoming projects.

Notable Performances

Alia Bhatt is renowned for her remarkable performances in various films, including "Highway", "Udta Punjab", "Raazi", and "Gully Boy". Her ability to portray diverse characters with depth and authenticity has earned her widespread acclaim.
